Larry Page

Co-founder of Google

Timeline of His Life at Google

  • 1998: Google was founded by Larry Page and Sergey Brin
  • 1999: Created smaller servers in hopes to make the search engine run faster and it worked!
  • 2001: Stepped aside to become the President of Products
  • 2005: Embarked on an ambission to make Google available everywhere through Android
  • 2011: Was appointed CEO for the second time

Page continued to improve Google and eventually step down from being the CEO in 2019.
Google would not be what it is now without Larry Page.

"If you’re changing the world, you’re working on important things".

-- Larry Page
